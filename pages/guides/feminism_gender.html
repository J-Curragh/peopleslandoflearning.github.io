In attempting to understand homophobia, transphobia, the patriarchy and other topics and issues related to gender, it is important to apply a dialectical materialist methodology, as explained by our basic theory guide. Asking questions like "if things were not always this way, what material and social conditions led to our current situation?" seems like a very good place to start. If you have any questions or criticisms left unanswered by the resources in this guide, please do not feel afraid to contact us and ask about it!
